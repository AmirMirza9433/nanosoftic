- **Use Cases:** logo highlight, title text, CTA buttons, cover glow effects

### Surface / Card Colors (for social creatives)
- **Card Soft:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.05)`
- **Card Muted:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.04)`
- **Border:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.12)`
- **Subtle Border:** `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08)`

### Quick Color Usage Guide
- **60%** dark backgrounds (`#0A0A0F`, `#0F172A`)
- **30%** neutrals/text (`#F8FAFC`, `#94A3B8`)
- **10%** accent highlights (`#06B6D4`, `#3B82F6`)
- Buttons, links, highlights me cyan-blue gradient use karein.
- Over-saturated rainbow palettes avoid karein, brand clean aur premium rahe.
